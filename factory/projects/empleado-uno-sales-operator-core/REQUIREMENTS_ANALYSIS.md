# REQUIREMENTS_ANALYSIS — Sales Operator Core

See detailed PRD: `docs/sales-operator-core/PRD-001-sales-operator-core.md`.

Core requirements:
- Campaigns by product, territory, city, vertical, channel policy and budget.
- Lead discovery/enrichment with source evidence.
- Lead scoring and personalized attack plans.
- CRM/Funnel Core bridge for approved leads.
- Outbound queue that is fail-closed until channel policy is validated.
- Daily rollups and learning loop.
